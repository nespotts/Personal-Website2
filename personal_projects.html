Self-Balancing Robot

I developed the design, electronics, and software to allow this to balance on two wheels and navigate autonomously while avoiding obstacles on the Arduino (C++) platform using magnetic odometry, laser range finding, and a 9-Axis IMU. A Teensy 4.1 is used as the main microcontroller and interfaces with an Orange Pi Zero 2W running Ubuntu.

Sun-Tracking Solar Panel

I designed the articulating 4-bar linkage and rotation axis to control the position of a solar panel mounted to the roof of my camper van. An arduino along with two motors, photoelectric sensors, potentiometers, and switches are used to autonomously track the position of the sun and orient the panel perpendicular to the sun's rays to maximize output power. I also created a wireless remote to be able to control the mode and to retract the panel for traveling. I've been using it for 2 years.
